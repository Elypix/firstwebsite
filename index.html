2. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

by Nintendo Entertainment Planning & Development

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild was developed for the Nintendo Switch and the Wii U and is the latest part of the Zelda Game Series. In a gigantic and beautiful open world called Hyrule you can, in form of the main hero, Link, fight against all kinds of enemys, explore the map, collect resources and of course, follow the wonderful storyline, that leads you towards 4 citys in different biomes, each struggeling against a rioting titan that was supposed to help keeping the villain Ganondorf inside The Castle of Hyrule where he holds the princess called Zelda captive. And of course, your job is to soothe the 4 titans and to defeat the Ganondorf in order to save Princess Zelda.

My personal estimated playtime: 200-400 Hours

General Information About The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

  • Date of Publishment: March 3rd 2017
  • Publisher: Nintendo Entertainment Planning & Development
  • Platforms: Nintendo Switch & Wii U
  • Price: 69,99 Euro
  • Units sold: 21,500,000
  • Gamemodes: Singleplayer

Particularities

  1. The gigantic open world
  2. The amount of sidequests and overall possible sideactivities
  3. The great story
